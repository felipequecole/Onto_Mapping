Who are we?

We are students at Federal University of São Carlos, Brazil and researchers at Machine Learning Laboratory. Felipe Quécole is a third year undergraduate student in Computer Science, and besides the graduation he is also a member of SECOMP, the academic computer week, and member of the Tutorial Education Program (PET-BCC). Pedro Migliatti is also a third year undergraduate student in Computer Science and member of PET-BCC. He is one of the general coordinators of SECOMP. Romão Martines is a fourth year undergraduate student in Computer Engineering, marketing director of the National Congress of Computer Engineering (CONENC), and also one of the general coordinators of SECOMP.

Where To Find Us?


A Universidade Federal de São Carlos (UFSCar) foi fundada em 1968 e foi a primeira instituição federal de Ensino Superior instalada no interior do Estado de São Paulo.

O DC-UFSCar tem habilitado e colocado anualmente no mercado de trabalho cerca de uma centena de profissionais, que atuam em empresas e em Instituições de Ensino Superior.
